Mission

Software that respects
how small businesses work

We exist to build tools that make small business owners more capable — not more dependent on software they don't fully understand.

"Give every small business owner access to the kind of tools that used to be reserved for companies ten times their size — without the complexity, the cost, or the learning curve."
— Foundr Technologies

What we believe

Too much software is built by people who have never done the job the software is supposed to help with. The result is tools that are technically impressive but practically frustrating — full of features no one asked for and missing the ones that matter most.

We take a different approach. Before we write a line of code, we spend time in the environment. We understand the workflow, the daily pain points, the time pressure, and the context in which the tool will actually be used. Then we build the simplest thing that solves the real problem.

Our principles

🎯
Focus over features
We build one thing well rather than ten things adequately. Every feature we add must earn its place.
⚡
Speed from day one
If it takes more than a few minutes to learn, we haven't done our job. Our apps work the way people already think.
🔒
Data you can trust
Your business data belongs to you. We protect it with industry-standard security and never sell or share it.
🤝
Honest pricing
No hidden fees, no bait-and-switch tiers, no features locked behind an upsell. What you see is what you get.
📱
Mobile first, always
Small business owners work on their feet. Every app we build is designed for a phone, not a desk.
🔍
Underserved markets
We deliberately target industries that enterprise software has ignored. That's where we can make the biggest difference.

The long view

We're not trying to build the next unicorn. We're trying to build things that genuinely help people — and to do that sustainably, for a long time. That means being deliberate about what we build, careful about how we grow, and honest with the people who trust us with their business data.

If we do this right, the businesses that use our apps will run more smoothly, close their days with more confidence, and spend less time on the tasks that used to eat their evenings.

That's enough for us.
