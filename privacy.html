Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Foundr Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") builds mobile applications for small business owners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our apps, including LotteryTrack.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when using our apps:

  • Account information (email address, name, username)
  • Business information (store name, address, state)
  • Operational data entered through the app (ticket scans, inventory records, daily reports)
  • Employee usernames you create for your team
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely through Stripe — we do not store card details)

We also collect limited technical data automatically:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App crash reports and error logs (via Sentry)
  • App usage information to improve performance

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the features of our apps
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To send important notices about your account or our services
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • To improve our apps based on usage patterns

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following trusted service providers who help us operate our apps:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication infrastructure
  • Stripe — payment processing (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Sentry — crash reporting and error monitoring

Each provider is contractually required to protect your data and use it only to provide services to us.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and store records are permanently deleted. Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (TLS), secure authentication, and row-level security on all database tables to ensure your data is accessible only to you and your authorized employees.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are intended for use by business owners and their employees. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To make a request, contact us at the email below. Account deletion can also be performed directly within the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. Continued use of our apps after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
info@foundrtechnologies.com
