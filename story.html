Our Story

Built from a problem
we lived firsthand

Foundr Technologies started not in a boardroom, but on the floor of a small store — where we watched business owners spend hours on paperwork that should have taken minutes.

Where it began

Small business owners are some of the hardest-working people in any community. They wear every hat — manager, accountant, buyer, and employee — often all in the same day. But the tools available to them haven't kept up. Enterprise software is too complex. Generic apps don't fit their workflows. Spreadsheets break down the moment you add a second employee.

We saw this gap up close working alongside small retailers, and we asked a simple question: why doesn't software that actually fits this work exist?

So we built it ourselves.

How we got here

2024
The problem becomes clear Watching lottery retailers reconcile daily sales with notebooks and calculators, we realized no dedicated software existed for one of retail's most operationally demanding categories.
2025
LotteryTrack is born We launched the first version of LotteryTrack — a mobile app purpose-built for licensed lottery retailers. Barcode scanning, daily close-outs, inventory tracking, and team management, all from a phone.
2026
Foundr Technologies is founded With LotteryTrack live on iOS and Android, we formalized as Foundr Technologies — a company with a clear mandate: find the industries that software has left behind, and build the tools they deserve.

Where we're going

LotteryTrack is just the beginning. There are entire industries running on paper and habit because no one has built them the right tool yet. We intend to change that — one focused, well-crafted app at a time.

We're a small team that cares deeply about the people using what we build. That means no feature bloat, no enterprise pricing, and no learning curve. Just tools that work — the moment you open them.
